How do I reach these kids?

A text-based adventure

How will I play this stupid game?

• You are a coding master, that much is certain. But now you face your greatest challenge: teaching a class of rambunctious newbies how to program with the best of them.

• Harness your powers, summon your patience and prepare yourself for a grueling test of educational prowess, coding knowledge and crowd management.

• If you keep your wits about you, and pick the right responses, your score will increase. If you can get to 25 points before the dreaded final challenge, and keep a positive attitude, your students will achieve web design mastery, become rich and famous and you'll be rewarded by getting to do the whole thing all over again with a whole new class!

• But beware! Fail to raise your score above 25 before the final challenge, or adopt a negative attitude, and your students will fail the program and turn to lives of crime. Choose wisely ...
